About Us

Exqquisite Quality Construction Inc. was founded on the belief that superior craftsmanship, integrity, and clear communication are the foundation of every successful build.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality construction services that exceed client expectations while fostering safe and reliable work environments for our teams and tradespeople.

Our Values

  • Quality workmanship and attention to detail
  • Honesty, transparency, and fair pricing
  • Timely delivery and proactive communication
  • Respect for clients, neighborhoods, and the environment

History

Founded by Anthony Myers, Exqquisite Quality has completed a variety of residential and light commercial projects across the region. With a focus on craftsmanship and client experience, the company has grown through repeat business and referrals.
